Career highlights

Cristiano Ronaldo's career highlights include winning five Ballon d'Or awards, five Champions League titles, and becoming the all-time leading scorer for both Real Madrid and Portugal.


Manchester United: Won 3 Premier League titles, the Champions League, and the FA Cup. Real Madrid: Won 5 Champions League titles, 2 La Liga titles, and multiple other trophies. Juventus: Won 2 Serie A titles and a Coppa Italia.

Al-Nassr: Currently playing for the club in the Saudi Pro League. Individual Awards: 5 Ballon d'Or awards (2008, 2013, 2014, 2016, 2017).
