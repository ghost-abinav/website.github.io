Cristiano Ronaldo

About the GOAT :

CR7 is the nickname of Cristiano Ronaldo, one of the greatest football players of all time. Born on February 5, 1985, in Madeira, Portugal, Ronaldo is known for his incredible skills, athleticism, and record-breaking achievements. He has played for top clubs like Manchester United, Real Madrid, Juventus, and currently Al-Nassr in Saudi Arabia. Ronaldo has won numerous awards, including five Ballon d'Ors, and holds records for the most goals in the UEFA Champions League and international football.
